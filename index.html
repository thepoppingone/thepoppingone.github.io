My Story

I grew up in a humble family, that prides in honesty and hardwork. Technology stepped into my life when I first received my Windows 95 PC when I was 10 years old. I never looked back and started my tech journey, with my first step in getting a Diploma in Information Technology in Singapore.

Being an adventurous person growing up on the Little Red Dot (Singapore), has made me hungry for new and challenging experiences outside of my comfort zone. I interned in multiple startups (Burpple, Peopeo Design, Epic Creations, Juven.co) across different countries such as Singapore, Beijing(China), California(USA), Hong Kong(SAR China) to get a better understanding of city cultures (life/work).

I love food, reading books and passionate in understanding Hong Kong's cantopop culture.

My past employers were Govtech Singapore and StashAway Singapore where I began my career as a Devops/Platform Engineer

I am currently involved with developing platforms with HashiCorp's tools to improve developers' productivity at SPH Media Trust, and is very much interested in Devops/Platform work.
